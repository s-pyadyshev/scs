FREEDOM TO ACHIEVE THE EXTRAORDINARY

The biggest breakthroughs happen when the best minds are encouraged to think for themselves. We want you to be one of them.

A groundbreaking partnership

HS2 is the largest construction project in Europe. It will link the north and south of the UK for the first time in history.

SCS Joint Venture is constructing 13 miles of twin-bore tunnels on the HS2 route to its southern terminus at London Euston. It will run 6 state-of-the-art tunnel boring machines, of which 5 will run simultaneously.

A culture of innovation

We believe in giving people the freedom to solve big problems with big ideas.

At SCS JV, that means ensuring our employees have the opportunity to move around the project, work flexibly, collaborate with others and find new ways of working. This enables them to solve the hardest problems in construction and develop an industry-changing approach that will achieve groundbreaking results.

We place great importance on teamwork – whether you’re on the ground or in the office. And as a diverse, multicultural and mutli-disciplinary company, SCS is a place where you can grow both professionally and personally.

LATEST FROM THE PROJECT

2022 was a year of huge progress for the project, with a series of momentous milestones achieved and permanent HS2 structures taking shape across the route. We recently celebrated another major milestone, with over 1,000 apprentice starts now confirmed on our levelling up project.
