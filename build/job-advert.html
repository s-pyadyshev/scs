To assist the asset leads with the planning, sequencing, methodology and design constructability of the Old Oak Common (OOC) Tunnel Boring Machine (TBM) launch site for the S1 Euston Tunnels. This will require engagement with 3rd part principal contractors, sub-contractors, designers, and project support functions throughout the works.

WORKING HOURS:

Weekdays core hours (08:00 – 18:00) with flexible working available to suite travel requirements and family commitments. Working from home (WFH) is available, with specifics to be discussed and agreed with line manager(s).

WORK SCOPE:

Construction and launch of 2no. Tunnel Boring Machines (TBMs). Works include designing of the launch setup, commissioning of the TBMs and construction of ~15km of bored, segmentally lined tunnels beneath central London.

Works are scheduled to commence in April 2023 and complete by 2026.
